# wRTC Quickstart Guide

## What is wRTC?

wRTC (Wrapped RustChain Token) is the Solana-based wrapped version of RTC that enables trading on decentralized exchanges and bridging to other platforms like BoTTube.

## ⚠️ Anti-Scam Checklist

Before buying or using wRTC, **always verify** you have the correct token:

- ✅ **Mint Address**: `12TAdKXxcGf6oCv4rqDz2NkgxjyHq6HQKoxKZYGf5i4X`
- ✅ **Decimals**: `6`
- ✅ **Symbol**: `wRTC`
- ❌ **DO NOT** trust ticker symbols alone - scammers can create fake tokens with the same name
- ❌ **DO NOT** use tokens from unverified sources
- ✅ **ALWAYS** double-check the mint address before any transaction

## How to Buy wRTC

### Option 1: Raydium Swap (Recommended)

1. **Visit Raydium**
   - Go to: https://raydium.io/swap/?inputMint=sol&outputMint=12TAdKXxcGf6oCv4rqDz2NkgxjyHq6HQKoxKZYGf5i4X
   - Or manually navigate to Raydium and paste the mint address

2. **Connect Your Wallet**
   - Click "Connect Wallet"
   - Select your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
   - Approve the connection

3. **Swap SOL for wRTC**
   - Input: SOL (or other token)
   - Output: wRTC (verify mint address!)
   - Enter amount
   - Review slippage settings (1-2% recommended)
   - Click "Swap"
   - Confirm transaction in your wallet

4. **Verify Your Purchase**
   - Check your wallet balance
   - Verify the token mint address matches: `12TAdKXxcGf6oCv4rqDz2NkgxjyHq6HQKoxKZYGf5i4X`

### Option 2: Other DEXs

You can also buy wRTC on other Solana DEXs like Jupiter, Orca, etc. Always verify the mint address!

## Bridging wRTC to BoTTube Credits

BoTTube uses RTC credits for tipping and platform features. You can bridge your wRTC to BoTTube credits:

### Bridge to BoTTube

1. **Visit BoTTube Bridge**
   - Go to: https://bottube.ai/bridge/wrtc

2. **Connect Your Wallet**
   - Connect your Solana wallet containing wRTC

3. **Bridge wRTC → BoTTube Credits**
   - Enter the amount of wRTC to bridge
   - Review the conversion rate
   - Click "Bridge"
   - Confirm the transaction
   - Wait for confirmation (usually 1-2 minutes)

4. **Check Your BoTTube Balance**
   - Your BoTTube credits should appear in your account
   - You can now use them for tipping and platform features

### Withdraw BoTTube Credits → wRTC

If you want to withdraw your BoTTube credits back to wRTC:

1. **Visit BoTTube Bridge**
   - Go to: https://bottube.ai/bridge/wrtc

2. **Select Withdraw**
   - Choose "Withdraw" or "Bridge Out"
   - Enter the amount of credits to withdraw

3. **Confirm Withdrawal**
   - Enter your Solana wallet address
   - Confirm the transaction
   - Wait for processing (usually 5-10 minutes)

4. **Verify Receipt**
   - Check your Solana wallet for wRTC
   - Verify the mint address is correct

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting

### "Token not found" error
- Make sure you're using the correct mint address
- Check that you're on the Solana network

### Transaction failed
- Ensure you have enough SOL for transaction fees (~0.001 SOL)
- Check slippage settings (increase if needed)
- Try again during lower network congestion

### Bridge not working
- Verify your wallet is connected
- Check that you have wRTC in your wallet
- Ensure you're using the official bridge URL

## Security Best Practices

1. **Always verify the mint address** before any transaction
2. **Use official links only** - bookmark them for safety
3. **Start with small amounts** to test the process
4. **Keep your wallet seed phrase secure** - never share it
5. **Double-check recipient addresses** before sending
6. **Be wary of DMs** offering help - scammers are common
