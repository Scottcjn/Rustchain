## üåâ How to Bridge wRTC to BoTTube Credits

**BoTTube bridge:** https://bottube.ai/bridge/wrtc

BoTTube credits allow you to tip content creators using RTC on the BoTTube platform.

**Steps:**
1. Visit https://bottube.ai/bridge/wrtc
2. Connect your Solana wallet
3. Enter the amount of wRTC you want to bridge
4. Approve the transaction
5. Your BoTTube account will be credited with RTC for tipping

**Note:** The bridge converts wRTC ‚Üí RTC credits at a 1:1 ratio.

---

## üîô How to Withdraw (BoTTube Credits ‚Üí wRTC)

If you want to convert your BoTTube RTC credits back to tradeable wRTC:

1. Go to https://bottube.ai/bridge/wrtc
2. Select the "Withdraw" option
3. Enter the amount of RTC credits to withdraw
4. Enter your Solana wallet address
5. Confirm the withdrawal
6. wRTC will be sent to your wallet within a few minutes
