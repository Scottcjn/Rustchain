# Mining Hardware Haikus

Here are several original haikus about mining hardware, submitted for the RustChain bounty "[BOUNTY: 5 RTC each] Write a Haiku About Your Mining Hardware".

## Traditional Mining Hardware

**Silent Whirring**
Fans spin cool and GPU bright,
Hashes flow through metal veins—
Digital gold tonight.

**Rig in Basement**
Cards stacked in cooling array,
Churning numbers through the night,
Fortune in the fray.

**Mining Rig's Song**
Wires tangled, LEDs glow,
Whirring fans a steady beat,
Wealth from silicon grows.

## Vintage Mining Hardware

**Old Power Mac G5**
Curved case, vintage design,
PowerPC still mines true—
History refined.

**SPARC Workstation**
Sun logo bright, enterprise,
Legacy systems rise—
Blockchain pays respect.

**IBM Mainframe**
Big iron hums softly now,
Mainframe strength for crypto—
Old tech finds new way.

## Modern Mining Hardware

**ASIC Miner**
Chips humming hot and fast,
Electric veins aglow—
Bitcoin futures cast.

**Mining Farm**
Rows of rigs in endless line,
Computing power vast—
Digital mine.

**GPU Array**
Nvidia's green bright shine,
Render worlds and coins—
Parallel design.

## Nature & Mining

**Cooling Systems**
Fans blow cool, heat escapes,
Silence broken by power—
Digital landscape.

**Electric Flow**
Wires carry lightning's weight,
Transforming watts to worth—
Energy takes shape.

**Server Room**
Servers stand like silent sentinels,
Humming through the dark,
Creating digital wealth.

## Short & Punchy

**Hash Power**
Calculations fly,
Random numbers find their match—
Blocks unlocked now.

**Miner's Work**
Code and hardware meet,
Solving puzzles deep below—
Rewards await true.

**Blockchain**
Decentralized chain,
Miners work as one whole—
Trust by consensus.

## RustChain Specific

**Proof of Antiquity**
Old hardware proves its worth,
Vintage chips still strong—
Time honored, blockchain born.

**Rustchain Mining**
Rust code runs secure,
Hardware fingerprinted—
Trust in every block.

**Vintage Miner**
Grandma's computer now,
Mines the blockchain with pride—
Legacy renewed.

Each haiku captures the essence of mining hardware from different perspectives - the technical, the nostalgic, the industrial, and the poetic. All are original works submitted for the 5 RTC each bounty.
