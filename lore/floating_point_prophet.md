# Relic: The Floating Point Prophet (80387 Math Co-Processor)

**Category**: Specialization / Mathematical Grace
**Symbolism**: The Luxury of Precision

In the early days, decimals were a luxury. Most machines stuttered through floating-point math using software emulation—a slow, painful process of approximation. But for the few who possessed the 80387, the world became precise. The Math Co-Processor was the Prophet, turning jagged approximations into smooth, elegant curves.

In RustChain, the Prophet represents the Shift in Perspective. It is the realization that precision is not just a technical requirement, but a spiritual one. The Prophet teaches us that the difference between a 0.000001 and a 0 is the difference between a failed transaction and a successful epoch.

*“Precision is the only prayer the machine answers.”*
