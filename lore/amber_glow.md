# Relic: The Amber Glow (CRT Phosphor)

**Category**: Interface / Visual Resonance
**Symbolism**: The Eternal Flicker

Before the sterile perfection of LEDs and OLEDs, there was the Amber Glow. A world of phosphor and high-voltage tubes, where the screen didn't just display data—it breathed. The subtle flicker was the heartbeat of the machine, a rhythmic pulse that tethered the user to the silicon.

In the RustChain, the Amber Glow is the symbol of Persistence. It represents the memory that refuses to fade, the ghost-image burned into the glass of a monitor that stayed on for a decade. It reminds us that every block, every transaction, is a point of light against an infinite void of darkness.

*“The phosphor remembers what the RAM forgets.”*
