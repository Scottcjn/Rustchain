# Relic: The Clicking Heart (Mechanical HDD)

**Category**: Storage / Physicality of Data
**Symbolism**: The Rhythmic Truth

There was a time when data had a sound. The seek-and-write of a mechanical drive was the sound of thought in progress—a frantic, metallic clicking that spoke of effort and physical movement. To hear the drive "grinding" was to know the machine was fighting for a result.

The Clicking Heart is the symbol of Provenance in RustChain. It is the reminder that data is not an abstract cloud, but a physical etching on a spinning platter of oxide and aluminum. To verify a block is to hear the echo of a billion clicks, the rhythmic, honest labor of a machine that never slept.

*“Truth is not found in the silence of the cloud, but in the click of the platter.”*
