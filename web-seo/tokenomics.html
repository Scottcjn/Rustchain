RustChain Tokenomics

RTC is the native cryptocurrency of RustChain. It's earned through proof of antiquity mining and can be withdrawn, traded, or bridged to other blockchains. Understanding the tokenomics helps miners make informed decisions about their participation.

Inflation Model

RustChain uses a controlled emission model to balance sustainability with miner rewards:

  • Fixed Epoch Reward: 1.5 RTC distributed every epoch (24 hours)
  • No Block Rewards: Rewards are epoch-based, not per-block
  • Hard Cap: Maximum supply of 21,000,000 RTC ensures scarcity
  • Halving Schedule: Epoch rewards halve approximately every 4 years
