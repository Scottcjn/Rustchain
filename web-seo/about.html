About RustChain

RustChain is the first blockchain that rewards vintage hardware mining through a unique Proof of Antiquity consensus mechanism. Unlike traditional cryptocurrencies that favor modern, powerful hardware, RustChain celebrates computing history and rewards the preservation of vintage technology.

What is Proof of Antiquity?

Proof of Antiquity is a novel consensus mechanism that rewards miners based on the age and rarity of their computing hardware. Older and rarer systems earn higher rewards through:

  • Hardware Fingerprinting: Authenticates genuine vintage hardware through CPU architecture detection, ROM verification, and thermal profiling
  • Entropy Scoring: Measures timing characteristics unique to vintage processors (no two vintage systems drift the same way)
  • Anti-Emulation Checks: Prevents virtual machines and emulators from claiming vintage status

How Rewards Work

RustChain distributes rewards every epoch (24 hours). Your share is determined by:

reward = (epoch_pool √ó your_weight) / total_pool_weight

Where your_weight is calculated from:

  • Hardware multiplier (based on architecture and age)
  • Uptime bonus (continuous operation earns more)
  • Attestation count (reliable miners earn trust)

Security and Fairness

Proof of Antiquity is designed to be secure and resistant to cheating:

  • No VM Mining: Virtual machines and emulators are detected and rejected
  • Hardware Authentication: Genuine vintage hardware is verified through multiple methods
  • Anti-Cheating: Timing analysis detects simulation and falsified attestations
  • Transparent: All blockchain data is public and verifiable
