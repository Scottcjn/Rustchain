RustChain Hardware Guide

RustChain rewards vintage hardware mining through multipliers based on architecture, age, and rarity. Older and rarer systems earn significantly more. Use this guide to find your hardware and understand its mining potential.

Hardware Fingerprinting

RustChain uses hardware fingerprinting to verify authenticity and prevent VM mining:

CPU Architecture

Detects genuine CPU architecture (PowerPC, 68K, SPARC, x86)

ROM Verification

Checks for authentic ROM signatures and known emulator ROMs

Timing Analysis

Measures clock drift and timing characteristics unique to hardware

Entropy Scoring

Calculates entropy from timing variance

Anti-Emulation

Detects virtual machines and emulators (QEMU, VMware, etc.)
