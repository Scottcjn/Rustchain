## Performance Notes

- Traditional sea shanty rhythm (4/4 time, moderate tempo)
- Call and response format perfect for group singing
- "Early in the morning" can be sung with enthusiasm
- Emphasize "Way hey and up she rises" with gusto
- Bridge can be sung slower/more melodically
- Final chorus should build in volume and energy

## Technical References Included

- RustChain (blockchain name)
- RTC (coin/token name) 
- Proof-of-Antiquity (consensus mechanism)
- One CPU = One Vote (mining principle)
- PowerPC, Pentium II (vintage hardware examples)
- RISC/CISC (CPU architectures)
- Hash, blocks (blockchain terminology)
- ASIC/GPU mining critique (anti-industrial mining stance)
