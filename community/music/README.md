# RustChain Community Music

Welcome to the RustChain community music collection! This directory contains original songs, sea shanties, and musical tributes to our decentralized blockchain network.

## Current Music Bounty

**[CREATIVE BOUNTY: 15 RTC] Write & Record a RustChain Sea Shanty or Theme Song**

Create an original song about RustChain, mining on vintage hardware, or the Proof-of-Antiquity concept.

### Submission Requirements

- **Original composition** (lyrics + music)
- **Recorded audio** (minimum voice memo quality, produced tracks earn bonus)
- **Duration**: 30 seconds minimum, 3 minutes maximum
- **Content**: Must reference at least 2 of:
  - RustChain
  - RTC (RustChain Token)
  - Proof of Antiquity
  - Vintage hardware
  - 1 CPU = 1 Vote
  - Mining

### Suggested Genres

- **Sea Shanty** (highly encouraged!) - "What shall we do with a PowerPC G4, early in the morning..."
- **Folk/Acoustic** - Ballads about decentralized mining
- **Chiptune/8-bit** - Perfect for our vintage hardware theme
- **Lo-fi Hip Hop** - Beats with spoken word about blockchain
- **Country/Folk** - Songs about honest work and fair mining

## File Organization

### Audio Files
- Place audio files in appropriate format subdirectories
- Accepted formats: MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A
- Include artist name and song title in filename
- Example: `sea_shanties/captain_blockchain_the_rustchain_way.mp3`

### Lyrics
- Include lyrics as `.txt` files alongside audio
- Same naming convention as audio files
- Example: `sea_shanties/captain_blockchain_the_rustchain_way.txt`

### Directory Structure
```
community/music/
├── README.md
├── sea_shanties/
├── folk_acoustic/
├── chiptune_8bit/
├── hip_hop_beats/
├── country_folk/
└── other_genres/
```

## Submission Guidelines

1. **Create a subdirectory** for your genre if it doesn't exist
2. **Upload both** audio file and lyrics text file
3. **Include metadata** in a comment or separate file:
   - Artist name/handle
   - Song title
   - Genre
   - Recording date
   - Any collaborators
4. **Open a pull request** with your submission
5. **Reference the bounty issue** in your PR description

## Quality Standards

- Audio should be clear and understandable
- No copyrighted material (samples, beats, melodies)
- Family-friendly content only
- Embrace the RustChain community spirit

## Inspiration

Think vintage computers humming in basements, the democratic nature of 1 CPU = 1 Vote, the authenticity of Proof-of-Antiquity, and the maritime tradition of decentralized cooperation. Let the spirit of old hardware and new blockchain technology guide your creativity!

## Reward Distribution

Successful submissions meeting all requirements will receive 15 RTC. Exceptional productions may receive bonus rewards at community discretion.
