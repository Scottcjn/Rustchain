# Community Videos

Welcome to the RustChain community video directory! This is where we collect video submissions from the community, particularly for creative bounties and educational content.

## Current Bounties

### [CREATIVE BOUNTY: 5 RTC] Explain RustChain to Your Grandma (Video Under 60 Seconds) - Bounty #2071

**Reward:** 5 RTC base (+3 RTC bonus if grandma is in the video)

Create a video explaining RustChain in simple terms that anyone can understand. No technical jargon allowed!

#### Requirements
- **Duration:** Under 60 seconds
- **Must explain:** What RustChain is, what mining means here, why old computers get bonus rewards
- **No jargon:** Avoid terms like "attestation", "epoch settlement", "decentralized consensus"
- **Format:** Any creative format works - talking head, animation, whiteboard, puppet show, interpretive dance
- **Upload:** BoTTube preferred, or YouTube/social media with link

#### Bonus Opportunities
- **+3 RTC:** Include your actual grandma/grandpa/elderly relative
- **+3 RTC:** Especially creative presentation style
- **+3 RTC:** Makes RustChain sound fun and approachable

### How to Submit

1. **Create your submission folder:** `community/videos/bounty-2071-grandma-explanation/submissions/YOUR_USERNAME/`

2. **Include these files:**
   - `video.mp4` - Your actual video file (if under 25MB)
   - `description.md` - Brief description of your approach
   - `links.txt` - Links to hosted video (BoTTube, YouTube, etc.)

3. **Submit a pull request** with your folder

### Video Hosting Options

**Preferred:**
- BoTTube (decentralized, community-owned)

**Accepted:**
- YouTube
- Vimeo  
- TikTok
- Twitter/X
- Any platform with public viewing

### Judging Criteria

Videos will be evaluated on:
- **Clarity:** Can a non-technical person understand it?
- **Accuracy:** Is the explanation correct?
- **Creativity:** Unique or entertaining approach
- **Time limit:** Must be under 60 seconds
- **Accessibility:** Easy to follow and engaging

### Tips for Success

- **Test your explanation** on someone who doesn't know crypto
- **Use analogies** - compare mining to familiar activities
- **Show, don't just tell** - visual aids help understanding
- **Keep it conversational** - like you're actually talking to grandma
- **Practice timing** - 60 seconds goes by fast

### Example Good Analogies

- Mining = Solving puzzles to help keep the network safe
- Old computers getting bonuses = Like giving senior citizen discounts
- Blockchain = A record book that everyone can see but nobody can cheat
