# RustChain: The Proof of Antiquity Blockchain

## What is RustChain?

RustChain is an innovative blockchain project that uses **Proof of Antiquity (PoA)** as its consensus mechanism. Unlike traditional Proof of Work (PoW) that consumes massive energy, RustChain rewards miners based on the age and rarity of their hardware.
