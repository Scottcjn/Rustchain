Bounty Payout Ledger

Single source of truth for payout status across active bounties.

Status Definitions

  • Queued: approved and transfer submitted
  • Pending: in RustChain 24h pending window
  • Confirmed: pending window passed and transfer confirmed
  • Voided: canceled before confirmation

Operational Notes

  • RustChain pending confirmations are processed hourly.
  • Each payout update should include: bounty reference, wallet, amount, pending id, tx hash.
  • Keep this ledger updated for transparency and anti-fraud tracking.
