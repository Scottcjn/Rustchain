## Welcome Bonus Program

RustChain now offers welcome bonuses to onboard new miners and contributors\!

### Miner Bonuses

| Bonus | Amount | How to Claim |
|-------|--------|--------------|
| **First Attestation** | 10 RTC | Run a miner, complete first successful hardware attestation |
| **Real Hardware Bonus** | +5 RTC | Additional bonus if running on real (non-VM) hardware |
| **Vintage Hardware** | +10 RTC | Running on PowerPC G4/G5, 68K, SPARC, or other vintage hardware |

### Developer Bonuses

| Bonus | Amount | How to Claim |
|-------|--------|--------------|
| **First PR Merged** | 25 RTC | Get your first pull request merged to any RustChain repo |
| **First Bounty Completed** | +10 RTC | Complete your first bounty (on top of bounty reward) |

### Referral Bonus

| Bonus | Amount | How to Claim |
|-------|--------|--------------|
| **Refer a Miner** | 10 RTC | Bring a new miner who completes first attestation |
| **Refer a Dev** | 15 RTC | Bring a new dev who gets first PR merged |

### How to Claim

1. **Miners**: After first successful attestation, comment on this issue with your miner ID
2. **Devs**: After first PR merged, comment on this issue with your wallet ID
3. **Referrals**: Comment with both your wallet and the new user's wallet

### Notes
- One-time bonuses per wallet (no double-dipping)
- Bonuses stack (e.g., first attestation + vintage = 25 RTC)
- Discord also has chat bonuses — join the server\!
